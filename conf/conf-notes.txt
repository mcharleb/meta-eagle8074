
============== BUILDING EAGLE8074 COMPLETE IMAGE: ==================

build-eagle8074

Builds the Eagle partitions and LK signed image.

===============================================================

Targets to build:

eagle8074-cache-image    - Firmware partition
eagle8074-factory-image  - Factory reset partition
eagle8074-persist-image  - Persist partition
eagle8074-recovery-image - Recovery partition
eagle8074-userdata-image - Rootfs
lk                       - LK signed image
